Weapon Info:
Medium Bowgun
A weapon lost to time. Used to be manufactured and used by the Third Generation of Hunters.

Striking a balance between Light and Heavy Bowguns, the Medium Bowgun is a weapon made to be truly modular. It comes in three parts, the Frame, Barrel and Stock. These can all be mixed and matched to create a Hunter's preferred bowgun of choice. Whether they want to equip it with the Light Bowgun's Rapid Fire module or the Heavy Bowgun's Shield, the choice is theirs.

The weapon lost use due to its many weaknesses and the lack of the Third Generation Hunter's creativity, resulting in most hunters just opting to use the Light or Heavy variants.
