Weapon Info:
Hammer
One of the first weapons created to combat monsters, the Hammer has been present in all generations and is one of the most reliable weapons of all time.

Hammers are large, heavy weapons which are capable of dealing vast amounts of damage in a single combo. They, along with the Hunting Horn, exclusively deal Impact-type damage. Their tremendous size only slightly hinders mobility and Hunters are still able to run with them, unlike with the Great Sword and Lance.

However as a downside, their defensive capabilities are extremely limited. Hunters are limited to their dodges and their wits when it comes to where they position when using this weapon.
Even with all this tremendous power, the Hammer can still get stronger. Just like the Great Sword, this weapon can do charged attacks that can knock out monsters, forcing them to ground for other hunters to do their own attacks.
