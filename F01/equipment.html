Forgotten Armory

Bowguns are a hunter’s trusty companion since the days of the First Fleet. However, the middle child of the Bowgun tree, the Medium Bowgun, has long been consumed by the sands of time.
Praised for its modularity, a Medium Bowgun can be wielded like a Heavy Bowgun but have the speed and weight of a Light variant. However, the way of crafting this weapon has long been lost to time.
From top to bottom: Light Bowgun, Medium Bowgun, Heavy Bowgun

Mighty Morphers

The 2-in-1 package deal weapons. Sword? Axe? They got them all. Combining both offense and defense in one weapon, these weapons are sure to keep you safe in nearly every situation.
Includes: Tonfas, Charge Blade, Magnet Spike, Switch Axe, Accel Axe

Shields Up!

“Shields up, Ironbreakers!”, a war cry of every hunter that decides to carry a shield, whether that shield comes with your Lance or is attached to your Heavy Bowgun, their focus is to always be on guard.
Includes: Lance, Gunlance, Heavy Bowgun

Patience is a virtue

Some weapons require you to wait for the perfect opportunity to strike. Bob, weave, then SLASH and BONK to your heart’s content. You know what they say, the best things are worth the wait.
Includes: Great Sword, Longsword, Hammer

The Untouchables

When there is a need for speed, these are what you choose.
Equipped with the ability to constantly be out of harm’s way, while dealing constant Elemental damage and also supporting allies, these weapons are perfect for hunters who know how to position in a hunt and take advantage of the wildness of a monster.
Includes: Bow, Dual Blades, Insect Glaive, Light Bowgun, Medium Bowgun
