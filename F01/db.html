Weapon Info:
Dual Blades
Dual Blades are a weapon manufactured by the Blacksmiths in-between the First and Second Generation of Hunters.

Best for dealing elemental damage or applying status effects, the Dual Blades are wielded with a certain flair by any hunter who uses it, almost as if they are locked into a dance with each move. The weapon allows a hunter to go into "Demon Mode", increasing their attack and speed while also allowing them to do moves normally impossible. However, this drains their stamina eventually.

If they're lucky, with enough time in Demon Mode they can transition into Archdemon Mode. This allows them to keep the advantages of Demon Mode without the constant stamina drain.
Try hunting these monsters with this weapon!

Black Diablos

Silver Rathalos

Gold Rathian
