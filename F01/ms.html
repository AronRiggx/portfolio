Weapon Info:
Magnet Spike
A heavy, melee type weapon with the ability to switch between two different weapon modes, the Magnet Spike can deal both Slash and Impact damage to combat any monster that its user may encounter.

The key aspect of the weapon is its magnetism abilities, this comes from the large red and blue phials located inside the weapon. Naturally being a large, heavy weapon, it would be difficult to even handle or use in combat, but by manipulating the magnetic force in the weapon most of the weight is lifted, it becomes possible to move so fast that it would otherwise be deemed unnatural.

The user will also be equipped with a "Magnetic Field Gun" (磁界銃) which can be used to fire a small, magnetic sphere that sticks onto monsters, allowing the user to pinpoint a specific location to lock on to and use magnetic attraction to travel from a much greater distance, or even use repulsion force for a long distance evade.
