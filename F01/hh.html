Weapon Info:
Hunting Horn
First used by the hunters of the Second Generation, this weapon is the go-to weapon for Hunters that wish to support their party.

Similar to the Hammer in that they do Impact damage and are able to K.O. a monster when they connect with the head, the Hunting Horn is known for its versatility in applying buffs and constantly providing support to other Hunters in every way.

Each Horn has a different set of three colored notes. These notes can be combined in various ways to create various effects, such as Attack and Defense boosts, or to mimic items such as the Psychoserum.
Try hunting these monsters with this weapon!

Daimyo Hermitaur

Congalala

Chameleos
