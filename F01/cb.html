Weapon Info:
Charge Blade
A weapon utilizing two weapon forms, the Charge Blade was first used and manufactured by the Fourth Generation of Hunters.

Charge your attacks in Sword Form then consume these charges to power up your shield. Next, morph to Axe Form and use these charges to deliver a devestating Super Amped Element Discharge, or the SAED.
Try hunting these monsters with this weapon!

Tetsucabra

Nerscylla

Gore Magala
