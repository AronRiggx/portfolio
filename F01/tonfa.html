Weapon Info:
Tonfa
Tonfas are weapons made by the hunters and other helpers that are inside the Frontiers, a dangerous area forbidden to most hunters. These weapons are lightweight and offer decent mobility while drawn, as well the ability to launch the hunter into the air. Tonfas operate with two modes: normal mode and short mode. During normal mode, tonfas deal normal impact damage and are capable of performing a charge attack on the ground, as well as a spinning drop attack while in midair.

In short mode, impact damage is reversed and the hunter can perform swift attacks on the ground as well as a rolling kick attack midair. The latter allowing the hunter to remain airborne for an extended time.

Similar to the Insect Glaive's capabilities, Tonfa users are also capable of launching upwards to vault towards or away from a monster. They also make use of their own fighting style called the "Earth Style".
