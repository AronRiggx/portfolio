Weapon Info:
Lance
Wielded since the first generation, Lances are long weapons that can strike from a distance. The lance is always accompanied by a large shield which generally grants a powerful defense against most attacks.

Although slow and difficult to travel with, the weapon's damage output can be impressive. Lances have different moves, such as the upstab, forward stab, and charging attack.

Lances are one of the only weapons that can attack and block at the same time. They are also capable of executing Counters to mitigate damage and stamina reduction compared to just blocking a monster's attack.
Try hunting these monsters with this weapon!

Dire Miralis

Brachydios

Lucent Nargacuga
