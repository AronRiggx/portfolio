Weapon Info:
Heavy Bowgun
Heavy Bowgun, the ranged weapon of choice for those who wish to have as much firepower as they can, while still maintaining some distance. This weapon has been in use since the First Generation.

While much slower than their Light Bowgun cousins, they are the only ranged weapon that allows a shield to be attached, giving them guard capabilities. For an experienced gunner, this is more of a restraint since you can only attach a power barrel OR a shield.

Heavy Bowguns usually do not support elemental shots unlike their cousins, the Light Bowguns.
Try hunting these monsters with this weapon!
