Weapon Info:
Insect Glaive
Sting like a Rathian, soar like a Kut-ku. The Insect Glaive is a dlouble-ended weapon first used by the hunters of the Fourth Generation. It allows for hypermobility and flexibility in the air, perfect for catching those pesky Flying Wyverns and smacking them out of the air.

Coming with the rod is a giant insect called the Kinsect, which can be sent out to attack monsters, draining out an "essence" upon hitting and give it to the hunter upon returning. The hunter can either freely send out the Kinsect on a direct flight, or first shoot out a pheromone bullet at the monster that works as a "lock on" for the Kinsect to home in.

After a Kinsect has locked in, they can extract an essence and give it to the Hunter. Hunters can get red, orange and white essences from a monster's head, tail, and arms respectively. These essences will then provide buffs to a Hunter, increasing their combat prowess drastically.
