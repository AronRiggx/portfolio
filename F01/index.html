THE FAST AND THE FURR-IOUS

Palico

All hunters are accompanied by their trusty Felyne friends, the Palico! Sometimes two Palicos! (appears in most Monster Hunter games)

Seikret

Sometimes, hunters are also given mounts to traverse the fields faster. They come in many forms! Such as this bird wvyern, the Seikret, the best mount to roam the Forbidden Lands. (first appeared in Monster Hunter Wilds)

Palamute

Another example of a wonderful companion that aids the hunters of Kamura in battle! It can use tools and scrolls just to aid the hunter in battle. (first appeared in Monster Hunter Rise)

Shakalaka

Shakalaka are highly social creatures that live in large tribes, working under the leadership of the King Shakalaka. They have rarely been known to enter human society. (first appears in Monster Hunter 2)

Poogie

Poogie, the Poogie, is a mascot for almost every single Monster Hunter game. It follows you around and blesses you with good luck (maybe... or not. Nobody knows!). Don't forget to pet him before you leave for a hunt!
